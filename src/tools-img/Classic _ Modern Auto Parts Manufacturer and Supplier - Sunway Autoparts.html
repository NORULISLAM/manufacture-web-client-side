Your One-Stop Vehicle Parts and Accessories Partner

Searching for the parts for classic car, motorcycles and trucks that's built in the early1920's or later? Then you have the right place now! We have what you’re looking for. And if we don’t, we’ll build it for you.

01 Auto Parts Sourcing

Our experts are high-skilled and passionate about vintage autos and their maintenance parts. When you present us with an auto part request, they will identify it and source it for you at a fair price through our auto industry partners.

02 Molding Design for Your OEM Parts

Our mold design team develops 3D mold designs based on the auto part sample that you present to us. We use these designs to manufacture molds that are highly precise and capture all the structural details of the part.

We also can partner with qualified auto parts mold making factories to produce precise mold for parts fabrication.

03 Precision Parts Manufacturing

Once the molds are made, the auto parts are injection molded using high-precision techniques and machine synchronization. We then use electrical discharge machines to perfect the detailing of the parts where necessary. Finally, the complete parts are polished and chrome-plated.

04Strict Quality Inspection

Every part is carefully measured to ensure that its dimensions match the original sample design. We also confirm that the material was evenly spread during injection molding and that there are no structural flaws. Only flawless parts are approved for packaging.
